Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2â€“3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until well mixed. Add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is bubbling, pour in the eggs and tilt the pan to spread them evenly.
  4. Add fillings: When the eggs start to set, add your fillings to one half.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold the omelette and cook for another minute, then serve hot.
